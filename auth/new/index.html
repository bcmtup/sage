Register a new FORSAGE account

To register in FORSAGE x3 and FORSAGE x4 programs, in a browser with the Metamask extension installed, follow the referral link from your partner. Check your upline partner ID, change it if necessary. Then click "To register".

Next, you will need to confirm the transaction in the wallet and wait for confirmation in the Ethereum network. This usually takes from a few seconds to 15 minutes. After clicking the "Automatic login" button, you will be redirected to your personal cabinet.

xGold activation

Please note that the xGold program is activated separately after registration in the x3 and x4 programs.

To activate the first xGold slot in your cabinet, click on the purchase icon. Next, you will need to confirm the transaction in the wallet and wait for confirmation in the Ethereum network.

Upgrade of slot to higher level

For all programs, the conditions are the same: click on the purchase icon and confirm the transaction.

Register a new FORSAGE account

Go to this address: contract x3/x4. Connect your wallet to Etherscan by clicking “Connect to Web3” and choosing Metamask or your smartphone wallet. Open “2. registrationExt "and enter the following information there:

  • payableAmount (ether) — 0.05 ETH;
  • referrerAddress (address) - your upline partner's wallet address. Make sure you enter the correct wallet address - it will be permanently registered as an upline partner. These details cannot be changed later.

Then click "Write" and confirm the transaction in Metamask or your smartphone wallet. When paying for new slots, do not choose the slowest price profile on GAS to avoid transaction hang-ups.

Manual upgrade of programs FORSAGE x3 and x4

Go to this address: contract x3/x4. Connect your wallet to Etherscan by clicking “Connect to Web3” and choosing Metamask or your smartphone wallet. Open “1. buyNewLevel "and enter the following data there:

  • payableAmount (ether) - the amount of reward in ETH required to activate the desired site;
  • matrix (unt8) - matrix type: for х3 - 1, for х4 - 2;
  • level (unt8) - slot number.

The slot number and its cost must necessarily correspond to each other. If you specify inappropriate values, the smart contract will not skip the transaction.

Then click "Write" and confirm the transaction in Metamask or your smartphone wallet. Make sure your transaction is complete before committing the next one. This can be done on Etherscan. If a “Success” sign appears next to the transaction, you can proceed to the next one.

When paying for new slots, do not choose the slowest price profile on GAS to avoid transaction hang-ups.

Activation of the first slot in the xGold program

Participation in the xGold program is possible only if you have already activated the first slots in the x3 and x4 programs. It will not be possible to activate xGold without operating slots in these programs.

Open your wallet that you used to register in the x3 and x4 contract.

Click "Send". In the recipient field, enter the address of the xGold contract: 0x488e3a4Bbbb2386bA619Eed88319E807C3dDb6C2. Enter the amount 0.05 ETH. Click "Send" and confirm the transaction in your wallet. The first xGold platform will be activated in your personal account.

When paying for new sites, do not choose the slowest GAS price profile to avoid freezing the transaction.

Manual upgrade of the xGold program

Send in the same way the amount required for the next upgrade to the smart contract address xGold.

Make sure your transaction is complete before committing the next one. This can be done on Etherscan. If a “Success” sign appears next to the transaction, you can proceed to the next one.
